History of Generative AI

Generative AI has evolved rapidly over the past decades, from simple algorithms to advanced neural networks capable of creating text, images, and more.

1950s - Early AI

The foundation of AI research, exploring simple machine learning concepts.

1980s - Neural Networks

Introduction of neural networks, enabling computers to recognize patterns and learn.

2014 - GANs

Generative Adversarial Networks revolutionized AI by generating realistic images and data.

2020s - Modern AI

Advanced models like GPT and DALL-E capable of generating text, images, music, and videos.
